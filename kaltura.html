--- title: Get External URL for a Kaltura Video layout: main ---

This page will take a Kaltura media URL from MediaSpace and convert it to a URL that can be used outside of Blackboard. In order to find the Kaltura media URL:

  1. Log into MediaSpace with your NetID and password.
  2. Find the video you wish to use.
  3. Click on the title to open the video on its own page.
  4. Copy the URL from the address bar of your browser.
  5. Paste the URL into the form below.
  6. Press Submit and the new URL should load below the form.
